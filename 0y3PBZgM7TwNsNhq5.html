What to Expect

Our live gatherings are filled with vibrant worship, inspiring messages, and a welcoming community. You can expect:

  • Warm greetings from our hospitality team
  • Contemporary and traditional worship music
  • Engaging and relevant sermons
  • Opportunities for prayer and reflection
  • Fellowship time after the service

Parking Information

We offer free parking for all attendees:

  • Main parking lot located directly behind the community center
  • Additional street parking available on Faith Street and surrounding areas
  • Handicap parking spaces available near the main entrance
  • Bicycle racks provided for those who prefer to cycle

Accessibility

We strive to make our gatherings accessible to everyone:

  • Wheelchair ramps at all entrances
  • Elevator access to all floors
  • Assistive listening devices available upon request
  • Sign language interpretation provided (please contact us in advance)
  • Large print materials available
